Ebiripo

Description

Ebiripo is a steamed cocoyam dish common amongst the Yoruba people of Remo in Ogun state.

Cocoyam is peeled, blended, mixed and wrapped in leaves and cook until it's done. Ebiripo is best eaten with egusi soup.

Ingredients

  • Cocoyam
  • Moin-moin leaves
  • Palm oil
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
