Ojojo

Description

Ojojo (Water yam balls) is a very delicious Nigerian dish/snack.

They are made from grated water yam, onions and peppers. It can be enjoyed first thing in the morning or just as a snack or side dish. It is very simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Water yam
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Onion
  • Crayfish (Optional)
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Peel, wash and grate water yam
  2. Transfer grated water yam into a bowl and add chopped onions and peppers
  3. Add crayfish (optional)
  4. Mix well and add some salt to taste
  5. Heat a generous amount of oil in a pan or deep fryer
  6. With a wooden spoon, work in the mixture by stirring vigorously
  7. Turn heat down to medium before frying
  8. With your hands or table spoon, scoop ojojo mix into oil for frying
  9. Use a perforated spoon to scoop ojojo balls from oil(at a desired texture) onto a plate dressed with napkins
  10. Serve
